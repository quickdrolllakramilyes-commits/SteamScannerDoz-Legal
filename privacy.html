SteamScannerDoz Privacy Policy

SteamScannerDoz may store:

  • Discord User IDs
  • Discord Server IDs
  • Bot settings and preferences

SteamScannerDoz does not collect:

  • Steam passwords
  • Steam API credentials
  • Payment information
  • Personal financial information

Collected data is used solely to provide bot functionality.

Data is not sold or shared with third parties.
